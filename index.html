Hello World!

My name is Bradley Howlett. I grew up about an hour's drive north of Detroit. In May of 2018, I graduated from Grand Valley State University (Grand Rapids, MI) with a B.S. – double major – in Cell Biology and Behavioral Neuroscience. An emphasis on the sciences of life and mind is a modest ambition to live as fully human being in harmony with the rest of life — paraphrasing Aldous Huxley in his novel, Island.

Upon graduating, I started an independent freelance writing business, Bradley Howlett Services LLC. Soon, I will be rebranding with a different name. My freelance work is divided between three vendors: Straive, ContentWriters, and Hawkes Learning. A few months after starting the LLC, I took a position as Research Assistant on Dr. Kyle Burghardt's research team at Wayne State University.
